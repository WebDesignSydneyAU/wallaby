Frequently Asked Questions

What is the average cost of installing an above-ground pool?
The average cost of installing an above-ground pool can range anywhere from $1,500 to $5,000 depending on size and materials used. This does not include any add-ons like decking or landscaping.
How long does it typically take for a contractor to install an above ground pool?
The installation process usually takes between 1-3 days, depending on the complexity of the project. However, this timeline can be affected by weather conditions and any additional features you may want to add.
What types of materials are commonly used in constructing an above-ground pool?
Above-ground pools are typically made from three main materials: steel, resin (a type of plastic), and aluminum. Each material has its own advantages and disadvantages in terms of durability, maintenance requirements, and aesthetic appeal.
What kind of maintenance is required for an above ground pool?
Similar to in-ground pools, above ground pools require regular cleaning using a net and vacuum system. Water quality must also be maintained through chemical balance checks and adjustments. Additionally, the pool liner will need to be replaced every 5-9 years.
