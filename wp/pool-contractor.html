Frequently Asked Questions

What services does a swimming pool contractor provide?
A swimming pool contractor provides various services including designing, building, and installing both residential and commercial pools. They also handle repairs, renovations, and regular maintenance tasks such as cleaning and chemical balancing.
How to choose the right swimming pool contractor?
When choosing a swimming pool contractor, consider their experience, reputation in the industry, customer reviews, and whether they are licensed and insured. Also consider their design capabilities if you want something beyond a basic rectangular pool.
What should I expect during the construction process of a swimming pool?
The construction process typically includes several stages - design phase where you finalize your plans with the contractor; excavation where the ground is prepared for your pool; installation of plumbing systems; laying of concrete or other material for the structure of the pool; installation of any additional features like lighting or water features; filling up and testing of the newly built pool. This process can take anywhere from few weeks to several months depending on complexity of design and weather conditions.
