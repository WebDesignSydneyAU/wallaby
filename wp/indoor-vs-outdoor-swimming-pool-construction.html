Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference in cost between constructing an indoor and an outdoor pool?
The cost of construction can significantly vary depending on numerous factors such as size, materials used, location, design complexity etc. However, generally speaking, indoor pools tend to be more expensive than outdoor ones due to additional costs associated with ventilation systems and building structures.
How does maintenance differ for indoor and outdoor pools?
Outdoor pools may require more regular cleaning due to exposure to weather elements and falling debris from trees or plants. Indoor pools are not exposed to these elements so they typically stay cleaner longer but they do require proper ventilation systems to maintain humidity levels and prevent growth of mold or mildew.
Which type of pool would take less time to construct?
Typically, an outdoor pool will take less time to construct since it doesnt require the additional step of building a housing structure around it which is needed for an indoor pool. However, this can vary based on several factors including the complexity of design and local weather conditions during construction.
