Frequently Asked Questions

What credentials, licenses, or certifications do you have as a swimming pool contractor?
The contractor should be able to provide evidence of their professional qualifications such as a valid license from a recognized trade association and any relevant certifications. They should also be insured and bonded.
How many years of experience do you have in building swimming pools?
This depends on the individual contractor. Ideally, they should have several years of experience specifically in building swimming pools.
Can you provide references or examples of your previous work?
A reputable contractor will happily provide references from past clients and/or photos or case studies of their previous work. They may also invite you to visit completed projects.
What is included in your contracts and agreements?
All essential details including the cost estimate, payment schedule, timeline for completion, project design and specifications, type and quantity of materials to be used etc., should be clearly stated in the contract.
How do you handle changes or issues that arise during construction?
Unforeseen circumstances might occur which can lead to changes in design, materials or costs. The contractor should explain how such situations are handled including communication processes with client about these changes.
