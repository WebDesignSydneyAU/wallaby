Questions to ask before hiring

Questions to ask before hiring

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your experience with pool construction?
The contractor should have several years of experience working on a variety of pool projects. They should be able to provide examples and references.
Are you licensed, insured, and bonded?
The swimming pool contractor should confirm that they are licensed in accordance with local laws, and carry sufficient insurance and bonds to protect you from financial loss if something goes wrong during the project.
Can you provide a detailed estimate for the project?
A reliable contractor will give a transparent estimate outlining all costs including materials, labor, permits, etc. This helps avoid surprise charges later on.
Do you offer any warranties or guarantees on your work?
Ideally, the contractor will provide some form of warranty or guarantee offering repair or compensation if there are issues within a certain timeframe after completion.
