Frequently Asked Questions

What are the main services provided by swimming pool contractors?
Swimming pool contractors provide various services such as design and construction of new pools, renovation or remodeling of existing pools, regular maintenance and cleaning, installation of features like lighting or heating systems, repair services and sometimes even landscaping around the pool area.
How do I choose the right swimming pool contractor?
When choosing a swimming pool contractor you should consider their experience and reputation in the industry. Ask for references from previous customers to verify their work quality. Also make sure they are licensed and insured to protect yourself from potential liabilities.
Can swimming pool contractors provide custom designs according to my preferences?
Yes, most swimming pool contractors offer custom design services where they work with you to create a unique design that fits your specific requirements and preferences. This can include elements such as size, shape, depth,color scheme and additional features like waterfalls or jacuzzis.
