Frequently Asked Questions

What are some reputable fiberglass pool contractors in Nashville?
Some well-known fiberglass pool contractors in Nashville include Pool & Spa Depot, Pools of Fun, and the Fiberglass Pool Company. Always make sure to do your research and read reviews before choosing a contractor.
How much does it typically cost to install a fiberglass pool in Nashville?
The cost can vary significantly depending on the size and design of the pool, but on average you can expect to pay between $15,000 to $30,000 for a basic installation. This price may increase with additional features or custom designs.
What are the benefits of choosing a fiberglass pool over other types?
Fiberglass pools have several advantages including quicker installation time as they come pre-made from the manufacturer. They also require less maintenance compared to concrete pools since they dont need resurfacing or liner replacement. Plus, their smooth surface is more resistant to algae growth and potentially damaging pH levels.
