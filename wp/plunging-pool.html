Frequently Asked Questions

What is a plunging pool?
A plunging pool, also known as a plunge pool, is a small, shallow pool built for the purpose of lounging, wading and cooling off instead of for swimming and exercising. Some are big enough for several people to sit in; others are designed for only one person.
What are the considerations when installing a plunging pool?
When installing a plunging pool, one must consider available space, desired depth and diameter, access to plumbing and electricity for heating or filtration systems, soil conditions on your property, and local building codes or HOA restrictions.
How much does it cost to install a plunging pool?
The cost of installing a plunge pool can vary greatly depending on size, materials used, location of installation and any additional features like heating or water jets. On average though you could expect costs to start around $10k-$20k but this can rise significantly with high end finishes or complex site conditions.
