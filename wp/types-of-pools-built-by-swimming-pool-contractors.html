Frequently Asked Questions

What are the different types of pools a swimming pool contractor can build?
Swimming pool contractors can build various types including in-ground pools, above ground pools, indoor pools, infinity pools, plunge pools and lap pools.
What materials are commonly used by swimming pool contractors when building a pool?
Pool contractors typically use concrete (also known as gunite), vinyl liner, and fiberglass for constructing in-ground swimming pools. Above ground pools generally use steel or resin frames with vinyl liners.
How much time does it usually take for a swimming pool contractor to construct a pool?
The timeline depends on the type and size of the pool. It could range from a few weeks for an above-ground or vinyl-lined in-ground pool to several months for a custom-designed concrete or gunite pool.
What factors influence the cost of building a swimming pool by contractors?
Several factors influence the cost including size of the pool, materials used, complexity of design, whether its an in-ground or above ground construction, additional features like lighting or waterfalls, and local labor rates.
