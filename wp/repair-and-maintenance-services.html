Frequently Asked Questions

What types of pool repair and maintenance services do you offer?
We offer a wide range of services including pump repair, filter cleaning/replacement, leak detection and repair, chemical balance checks, regular cleaning service, tile replacement, and more.
How often should my swimming pool receive maintenance services?
It largely depends on how frequently the pool is used and its exposure to elements like leaves or dust. However, for optimal condition and longevity, we recommend weekly or bi-weekly professional maintenance visits.
How can I know if my swimming pool needs repairs?
Some common signs your pool may need repairs include changes in water level or color, cracking or flaking in the pools surface, unusual noises from the pump system, or decreased efficiency of the heating system. If you notice any of these issues its best to schedule a professional inspection immediately.
