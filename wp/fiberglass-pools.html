Frequently Asked Questions

What are the main advantages of fiberglass pools?
Fiberglass pools have several advantages including quicker installation, lower maintenance requirements, a non-abrasive surface, and they are less likely to require repairs compared to concrete or vinyl liner pools.
How long does it take to install a fiberglass pool?
The installation of a fiberglass pool typically takes between 3 and 6 weeks. This is significantly faster than concrete pools which can take several months to install.
How much does a typical fiberglass pool cost?
The cost of a fiberglass swimming pool will vary depending on size, location, and additional features such as lighting or heating. However, on average, you can expect to pay between $20,000 and $60,000.
What kind of maintenance do fiberglass pools require?
Fiberglass pools require less maintenance than other types of swimming pools. They need regular cleaning with pool skimmers and vacuum cleaners along with balanced water chemistry. Periodic acid washing is not required like in concrete pools.
