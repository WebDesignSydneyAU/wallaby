Frequently Asked Questions

What qualifications and experience does the swimming pool contractor have?
The contractor should be licensed, insured, and have several years of experience in installing or repairing swimming pools. They should also provide references of past jobs that you can verify.
How does the swimming pool contractor handle project planning and design?
The right contractor will work closely with you to understand your needs and preferences. They should offer expert advice on design elements while considering your budget. They should also provide a clear timeline and blueprint for the project.
Does the swimming pool contractor offer warranties or guarantees on their work?
A reputable contractor will stand behind their work by offering warranties or guarantees. This provides assurance that if there are any issues with the work completed, they will be resolved at no additional cost to you.
