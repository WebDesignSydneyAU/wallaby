Frequently Asked Questions

What is an infinity edge in the context of spas and swimming pools?
An infinity edge, also known as a vanishing or negative edge, is a design feature where one edge of the pool appears to merge with the surrounding landscape or seems to extend into the horizon. This creates a visually stunning effect of water with no boundary.
How does an infinity edge pool function and what differentiates it from traditional pools?
Infinity pools have a catch basin or trough where water spills over from the main pool. A pump then recirculates this water back into the main pool. This system differs from traditional pools that dont have this overflow feature.
Can you incorporate fountains into an infinity-edge spa or swimming pool design?
Yes, fountains can be incorporated in any type of pool or spa design including those with infinity edges. They can add aesthetic appeal and create relaxing sounds that enhance your swimming experience.
What are some considerations when installing an infinity-edge spa or fountain in my backyard?
Some important considerations include your budget (as these features tend to be more expensive than standard ones), space availability (since they require extra room for their catch basins), and maintenance needs (as they may require more upkeep due to their complex circulation systems).
What special services do you offer as a contractor for installing infinity edges, spas, and fountains?
As a professional contractor, we offer comprehensive services which include initial consultation and design ideas, site analysis, installation of all plumbing and mechanical systems needed for these features, construction of the main structure itself followed by testing and maintenance instructions upon completion.
