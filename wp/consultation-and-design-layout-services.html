Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in your consultation and design layout service?
Our consultation and design service includes an initial meeting to understand your requirements, site analysis, conceptual design creation, detailed drawings with measurements and specifications. We work closely with our clients throughout this process to ensure their vision is accurately reflected in the final design.
How do you price your consultation and design layout services?
The cost of our consultation and design services will depend on various factors such as complexity of the project, size of the area, specific client requests among others. After an initial meeting where we discuss these variables, well provide a detailed quote outlining all associated costs.
Can I make changes to the design once its been finalized?
Yes, while we aim to incorporate all your needs during the designing stage itself, we understand that changes may be required later on. However, please note that significant alterations after finalizing could potentially lead to additional costs and extend project timelines.
