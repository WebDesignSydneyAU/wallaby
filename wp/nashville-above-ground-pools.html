Frequently Asked Questions

What types of above ground pools do you offer in Nashville?
We offer a variety of above ground pools, including steel frame, resin frame, and hybrid pools. Our selection also includes different shapes like round, oval, or rectangle to fit your backyard space.
How long does it typically take to install an above ground pool in Nashville?
The installation process varies based on the size and type of the pool but generally it takes between 1-3 days. However, this doesnt include the needed time for necessary permits or preparing the site.
What are the maintenance requirements for an above-ground pool in Nashville?
Regular maintenance for an above-ground pool includes testing and balancing water chemicals, cleaning pool walls with a brush, vacuuming the floor, skimming debris off the surface and maintaining filter system. Frequency can depend on usage and weather conditions.
Is any special permit required to install an above ground swimming pool in Nashville?
Yes, according to local codes in Nashville you need a permit to install any kind of swimming pool. You will need to submit plan drawings and other documentation as part of your application. Its best to consult with us or a local authority for specific details.
