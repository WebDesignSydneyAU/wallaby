Frequently Asked Questions

What is a plunge pool and how does it differ from a regular swimming pool?
A plunge pool is a small, shallow pool thats typically used for lounging, wading, and cooling off instead of swimming. They occupy less space and use fewer resources than traditional pools, making them an ideal choice for smaller yards.
How much space is required to install a plunge pool?
The size of the plunge pool can vary based on your requirements but they generally range from 4m x 2.5m to 7m x 3m. Hence, you would need at least this amount of free yard space for installation.
What are the costs involved in building a plunge pool?
Costs may vary greatly depending on the materials used, location, labor cost and other factors. On average though, expect to spend anywhere between $10,000 to $20,000 or more for installation.
How long does it take to install a plunge pool?
The time frame depends on several factors including complexity of design and weather conditions; however typically it takes around four weeks from excavation to completion.
What type of maintenance does a plunge pool require?
Just like traditional pools, plunge pools require regular maintenance including water testing and balancing as well as cleaning the filters and surfaces. However due to their smaller size they might be easier and cheaper to maintain than larger pools.
