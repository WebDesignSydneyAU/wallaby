Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum size for a small swimming pool that you can construct?
A: The minimum size we often recommend for a small swimming pool is around 10 x 20 feet, but we can customize based on your specific needs and space availability.
How much will it cost to build a small swimming pool?
The cost varies widely depending on the design, materials used, location, and other features. However, typically, a small basic pool could cost anywhere from $15,000-$25,000.
How long does it take to install a small swimming pool?
Generally speaking, the entire process from excavation to filling water may take anywhere between 8-12 weeks. This timeline can vary based on weather conditions or any custom features being added.
Do I need any special permits to install a small swimming pool in my backyard?
Yes, most municipalities require permits for installing pools. We assist our clients with this process by ensuring all necessary paperwork and inspections are completed as required by local laws.
